Why use this app?

In order to learn about the changes in the body after SCI and to best care for yourself or a loved one or friend who is living with an SCI.

Web App developed by Marwa Mekki, MD and George Marzloff, MD

We recommend adding this app
as an icon to your Homescreen! (Find in your Bookmark options)

Thank you for using this app. It is designed to be a quick reference for patients with spinal cord injuries and their families.

Disclaimer: The information presented here is not intended to replace the advice of a medical professional who you should consult regarding medical concerns and questions.
