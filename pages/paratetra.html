Type of Injury
Paraplegia and Tetraplegia

"-Plegia" means paralysis. Depending on the level of the injury in the spinal cord, SCI can cause "tetraplegia" (tetra=four) where four limbs are affected, both arms and legs; or "paraplegia" (para=2) where only legs that are affected by paralysis.

Tetraplegia: Injuries to the cervical levels, formerly called "quadriplegia" (quad=four).

Paraplegia: Injuries to the thoracic, lumbar, and sacral levels
