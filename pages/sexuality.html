Sexuality after SCI
What to expect

Because of the change in muscle movement, sense of touch, and reflexes after a spinal cord injury, your sexual experience could be different after a spinal cord injury. How you experience arousal and orgasm, and questions of fertility can depend on the level of injury and whether the injury is complete or incomplete

Loss of movement or sensation does not change the fact that you are a desirable sexual person

Understanding and getting comfortable with the new way your body feels and moves can help you start to explore your sexuality

A sexual life after spinal cord injury can be fulfilling and feel good
